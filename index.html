Oracle

Oracle (ORCL) has been in the news lately for several reasons. The company's AI forum highlighted challenges in its cloud business, causing its stock to fall. However, Oracle is still seen as a winner from the AI boom, with strong growth in its cloud infrastructure division and plans to build superclusters with Nvidia chips. Additionally, Oracle has partnered with Nvidia to offer a new cloud service for training and deploying generative AI models. Finally, Oracle was named a Leader for the eighth consecutive year in the 2023 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Cloud HCM Suites for 1,000+ Employee Enterprises for Oracle Fusion Cloud Human Capital Management (HCM).

Lululemon

Lululemon Athletica (LULU) has outperformed Nike (NKE) in the stock market over the past five years, with a 200% return compared to Nike's 46% gain. Lululemon is a growth stock that investors should buy and hold, even in a market downturn, due to its strong underlying business performance. Lululemon, a popular athletic apparel company, saw a slight decrease in its stock price during the most recent trading session. The stock closed at $396.70, which represents a decline of 1.65% compared to the previous day's closing price. Lululemon made its debut on the S&P 500 Wednesday morning. The company has made efforts to expand globally with 35 new international stores, most of which will be located in China.
